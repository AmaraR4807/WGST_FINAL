Activism and Organizations

Activism has been a huge part of LGBTQ+ history. From the Stonewall Riots to modern day protests and advocacy, LGBTQ+ individuals have fought for their rights and visibility. Many organizations have been established to support LGBTQ+ rights, provide resources, and advocate for policy changes. Here are some notable organizations:
